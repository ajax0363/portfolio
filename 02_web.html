02_web

Intro

02_web was our first theme. we were introduced to the fundamental and most applied tools working as a multimedia designer. these tools were soon shown to compose the fundament for the rest of the education. we were introduced to the fundamental professionnel concepts within design, digital user interfaces, digital content production, digital communication and responsive design. we learned to create websites using html and css and this was were we got our first hands-on assignment within graphic, photoshop skills, text set-up and using pictures in adobe XD.

Responsive site

this theme, 02_web introduced us to working with principles within basic design and programming of digital solutions - with a special focus on building a website and the structure of the user interface. we learned the importance of sketching, creating a moodboard, a styletile, a lo-fi wireframe and the rhyme of a functional layout. not to mention the importance of color, fonts, composition, and contrast.
