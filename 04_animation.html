04_animation

Intro

04_animation introduced us to javascript, which were soon shown to be a necessity to coding advanced and dynamic websites as well as creating interactive elements such as: burgermenus, slideshows, games etc. we focused on the fundamental principles within design and the programming of digital solutions - especially looking at the structure and layout of the userface. we analyzed game-design and how it engaged the user through UI-elements, character-elements, background-design and composition techniques.

Catch the beer!

this theme focussed on the creation of a game using javascript and css animations. we had to come up with a concept and an idea and we had to design all elements from the bottom. 04_animation focused on the journey working with shaping of graphic elements and making them work in practise. we were introduced to the importance of working with an activity- and state machine-diagram - which is used for planning the complicated interactive flow in the game. furthermore sketchingteniques, moodboards and styletiles.
