03_UX

Intro

03_UX shoved us that UX-design is a solid component of the development of digital user interfaces and the workareas, tools and methods are used in all of the five themes this first semester. 03_UX has given us a fundamental understanding of the interaction between user and user interfaces, furthermore witch theories, tools and methods are used for researching, designing and testing in a digital product-development. we have learned that the understanding of this interaction is very important for the future designchoices and product-development. they are based on concrete insights about the users instead of immediate assumptions.

ACEWEAR

we have worked with UX-methods and were giving an understanding regarding the presentation of our product and how to pitch our research- and test-results for a potential client, the best way possible. in 03_UX we focused on research-methods, analyzing sites, and content-development - especially copywriting and furthermore UI conventions, prototyping and testing. based on this knowledge we designed and created our own website in adobe XD focusing on user experience, copywriting, UI-elements and creating a functional website.
