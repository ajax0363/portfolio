05_indhold

Intro

in the theme 05_content we worked with design, programming and production of simple digital content solutions to selected media platforms. these solutions were designed, programmed and produced focusing on content- and business strategy. 05_content gave us a fundamental introduction to videoproduktion, including preparatory work before the actual shoot, the actual shoot and the post-production. we were introduced to premiere pro and audition for editing both video and audio and we learned the basic terms and elements within video- and photoproduction. 05_content taught us about color grading and crop of different frames, using microphones, user-profiles and different test, including BERT- and 5 sec test.

Redesign

we used our previously-learned skills to redesign a chosen website and we chose a sandwich-café and made them a brand new website. we also learned the importance of good communication and how important it is to have the skills to make small video- and photoproduction. we made analyzed the existing website trying to get a sense of perspective using different test and later on we started making styletiles, looked at the photostyle, colors, fonts etc. furthermore we made moodboards, storyboards, interview guides, wireframes, layoutdiagrams and prototypes, to figure out the new design of the website.
